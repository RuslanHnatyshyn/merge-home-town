How to Play

Merge and Create

Combine everyday items like furniture, tools, and decorations to craft new, unique pieces that enhance your town.

Rebuild Your Town

Restore homes, parks, and community buildings by merging resources and unlocking exciting new structures.

Complete Tasks

Help your town’s residents with tasks and puzzles to unlock rewards and new areas.

Expand Your Land

Discover new parts of the town as you progress through levels, from cozy neighborhoods to bustling city centers.

Customize Your Town

Personalize your town by choosing decorations, landscaping, and building styles to make it truly yours.

About the Game

Merge Home Town: Merge Games is a fun and relaxing puzzle game that lets you build, design, and restore a charming town. By merging everyday items, players can create new tools, decorations, and structures, bringing life back into their communities.

Whether you’re restoring homes, building new shops, or decorating parks, every part of your town reflects your creativity. With easy-to-learn mechanics, a vibrant world, and endless customization options, Merge Home Town: Merge Games is the ultimate blend of merging puzzles and town-building fun.
